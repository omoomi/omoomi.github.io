Privacy Notice

For omoomi Free Early Access AI Companion · Version 2.0 · Effective [to be inserted]

This Privacy Notice describes how omoomi Inc. ("omoomi", "we", "our") collects, uses, and protects personal information when an Account Holder and a Care Subject use the omoomi Free Early Access AI Companion (the "Service") in Taiwan. It is written for the Account Holder, who is typically a family caregiver supporting a parent or other loved one.

1. Who we are

omoomi Inc. is a Delaware corporation operating the Service in the Republic of China (Taiwan) through omoomi Inc. Taiwan, 5F., No. 150, Sec. 2, Nanjing E. Rd., Zhongshan Dist., Taipei City.

2. Scope of this notice

This notice covers the Free Early Access version of the omoomi AI Companion, including conversational companionship, gentle reminders, life story collection, and the long-term memory that allows omoomi to remember context across conversations. Future features, including Daily Digest, Ask omoomi, voice messages, care helper updates, and family dashboard features, will be governed by an updated notice when they ship.

3. Information we collect

3.1 Information you provide

  • Account information: your name, email address, phone number, and password.
  • Care Subject profile: the Care Subject's name, relationship to you, year of birth, language preferences, and any context you choose to share (interests, routines, family members).
  • Conversations: the words spoken or typed by the Care Subject during their interactions with omoomi, and any questions you ask omoomi about the Care Subject when that feature ships.
  • Voice messages: short audio clips you record for the Care Subject (when this feature ships).

3.2 Information generated by the Service

  • Conversation summaries and, when available, daily digests created by omoomi from the underlying transcripts.
  • Long-term memory entries: structured facts about the Care Subject extracted from conversations (stories, preferences, recurring topics).
  • Service usage events: timestamps, feature interactions, and basic device information needed to deliver and improve the Service.

3.3 Information we do not collect

  • We do not connect to biometric sensors or wearable devices in this early-access release.
  • We do not record continuously in the background; omoomi listens only when activated.

4. How we use information

  • To provide the Service: keeping the Care Subject company, supporting gentle reminders, preserving life stories, and remembering context over time.
  • To improve the Service: aggregated, de-identified analytics to understand which features help families most.
  • To communicate with you: account messages, security notices, and (with your consent) product updates.
  • To meet legal obligations under the Personal Data Protection Act of the Republic of China.

We do not use personal information for behavioral advertising, and we never sell elder-care data to any third party.

5. Legal bases & consent

We rely on your consent, the necessity of performing our agreement with you, and our legitimate interests in operating and securing the Service. Where Taiwan's Personal Data Protection Act applies, we collect and process personal information consistent with its requirements, including specific informed consent before recording or analyzing voice content.

6. AI processing & long-term memory

omoomi is an AI companion. Conversations are transcribed and processed by large language models to generate summaries, answers, and memory entries. Long-term memory persists across sessions so that omoomi can refer back to past stories and preferences in future conversations.

We do not use Care Subject conversations to train foundation models operated by third parties. Where model inference is performed by a third-party processor, we contractually prohibit re-use of the content for training. A current list of subprocessors is available on request.

You may review, edit, or delete long-term memory entries at any time from your account.

7. Sharing & disclosure

We share personal information only with:

  • Service providers acting on our written instructions (cloud hosting, model inference, transactional email, customer support).
  • Other account members you authorize (for example, a sibling you add to the family view when that feature ships).
  • Authorities, when required by Taiwan law or to protect the vital interests of the Care Subject.

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

10. Your rights

Under Taiwan's Personal Data Protection Act, you may:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you and the Care Subject.
  • Correct information that is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Delete personal information, subject to retention required by law.
  • Withdraw consent to optional processing at any time.
  • Object to processing or restrict certain uses.
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant Taiwan authority.

To exercise these rights, use the contact details below. We respond within 30 days.

11. Security

We use industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS 1.2 or higher) and at rest, role-based access controls, and audit logging. No system is perfectly secure; we encourage you to use a strong, unique password and enable multi-factor authentication once available.
