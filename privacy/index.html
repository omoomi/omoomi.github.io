Privacy Notice

This Privacy Notice explains how omoomi Inc. ("omoomi", "we", "our") handles personal information on this website and for the Guardian Hub waitlist. This notice is intentionally narrow: it covers our public website, early access interest forms, and related communications.

1. Who we are

omoomi is building Guardian Hub, an AI companion experience for family care. You can contact us at support@omoomi.io.

2. What we collect

  • Email address you submit through the waitlist form.
  • Messages or requests you send to us by email.
  • Basic website analytics, such as pages visited, approximate location, device type, browser, and referral source.
  • Technical data needed to operate and protect the website, such as IP address, timestamps, and server logs.

3. How we use it

  • To manage the Guardian Hub waitlist and send early access or product updates.
  • To respond to questions or support requests.
  • To understand website traffic and improve the website.
  • To protect the website, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use waitlist emails for behavioral advertising.

4. LaunchList

Our waitlist form is processed by LaunchList at getlaunchlist.com. When you submit your email address, it is sent to LaunchList so they can help us manage the waitlist. LaunchList acts as a service provider for this purpose.

5. Sharing

We share personal information only with service providers that help us run this website and waitlist, such as LaunchList, Google Analytics, hosting providers, and email or support tools. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the public.

6. Retention

We keep waitlist emails for as long as needed to provide launch and early access communications, unless you ask us to delete your email earlier. Website analytics and server logs are kept for a limited period according to the settings of our analytics and hosting providers.

7. Your choices

You can ask us to remove your email from the waitlist, unsubscribe from updates, or delete personal information you provided by contacting support@omoomi.io.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect personal information. No website or online service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Notice as the website, waitlist, or Guardian Hub evolves.

10. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Notice can be sent to support@omoomi.io.
