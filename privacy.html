Your child's privacy isn’t fine print. It’s our foundation!

We believe that what happens at home should stay at home. omoomi processes information locally and gives you complete control over your data.

What We Collect & Why

We collect only the data necessary to operate omoomi. Here’s a transparent breakdown of the information we handle and why it’s required.

Data Purpose Retention
Device Usage Stats To personalise content recommendations based on play patterns. Stored locally; deletable by parents
Parent Email To send updates and resource guides. Stored securely until you unsubscribe

Control & Consent

We give parents full control over data through an intuitive dashboard. From there you can view usage, download records or delete everything with one click.

Minimal Data Collection

We collect the bare minimum needed to make play meaningful. If it’s not essential, we don’t ask for it.

Easy Deletion

Delete your data at any time without contacting support. Your choices are immediate and respected.

Clear Consent

We never collect sensitive information without explicit, informed parental consent.

Compliance & Transparency

omoomi is designed to meet COPPA and GDPR requirements for children’s privacy. We communicate changes directly and in plain language.

  • COPPA/GDPR compliant infrastructure and policies.
  • No hidden data collection or sharing with third parties.
  • Regular updates communicated to parents directly.
