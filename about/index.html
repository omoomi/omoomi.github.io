Our story

We built omoomi because we know what it's like to love someone from 80km away.

Millions of family caregivers are already keeping watch, at 11pm, between meetings, after the kids are in bed.

They don't need more data. They need presence: someone to keep their loved one company when they can't be there.

We started with the daily moments families miss most: conversation, reminders, and stories.

What we believe

Elder care is one of the most intimate and important things a family does together. We build our product and company around three beliefs:

  1. 01
    Elders deserve dignity. Not just safety. Not just convenience. Real, continued dignity in how they're seen, heard, and supported.
  2. 02
    Caregivers deserve peace of mind. Not just information. Actual peace, the kind that lets you sleep, work, and be present in your own life.
  3. 03
    Technology should earn trust, not assume it. That means being transparent about what omoomi does, honest about what it can't do, and relentless about getting better.
