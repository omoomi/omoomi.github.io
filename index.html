AI Companion

Designed for senior caregiving conversations. Guardian Hub understands hesitation, repetition, and emotional subtext.

When your loved one trails off mid-sentence or repeats a story from yesterday, Guardian Hub listens the same way a patient friend would. They feel heard, not corrected.

Gentle Reminders

Guardian Hub reminds like a person, not an alarm. If your loved one says "not now," Guardian Hub circles back later with a softer nudge.

Works for everyday routines like hydration, movement, appointments, and medication reminders, but not emergencies or medical decisions.
