Meet omoomi

omoomi is an AI-powered companion designed to nurture your child's curiosity, creativity, and emotional well-being. Unlike screens and tablets, omoomi encourages hands-on play, meaningful conversations, and real-world learning, all while giving parents peace of mind with privacy-first technology.

From bedtime stories to interactive games, omoomi adapts to your child's unique interests and developmental stage, making every interaction engaging, educational, and safe.

Your Child's Privacy Matters

We believe what happens at home should stay at home. That's why omoomi works completely offline, no internet needed after initial setup. Your child's conversations, laughter, and learning moments stay private, with nothing recorded, stored, or shared externally. Just pure, protected playtime that gives you complete peace of mind.

Our Story

omoomi was founded by a team of entrepreneurs and technologists passionate about children's development. We saw first‑hand how excessive screen time and rigid toys hamper curiosity.

Working with early childhood educators, psychologists and parents, we're designing an AI companion that balances learning with play. Our offline‑first, privacy‑centric approach means your child can explore freely without compromise.

Our mission is to shape screen-free childhood solutions, help working parents get some relief without the guilty, and support real learning through play.
