Meet the Team Behind Eddie

omoomi was founded by a cool aunt and fun uncle who saw first-hand how screens limit genuine connection. We're building Eddie, an AI Bond Weaver that brings families together through real conversations and authentic moments that matter.

Join Pioneer Program

Our Vision

We envision a world where every parent feels empowered and supported in their role as their child's first teacher. With Eddie, the AI Bond Weaver, families rediscover the joy of being present together, exploring their world with wonder and building bonds that last a lifetime.

Our Mission

Our mission is to transform everyday moments into opportunities for connection, curiosity, and growth. We're building a future where AI empowers families to better navigate and engage with the physical world around them, free from screens and filled with confidence.

Our Team

Behind Eddie is a diverse team of early‑childhood educators, psychologists, AI engineers, and designers, who are also parents themselves. We collaborate closely with child‑development specialists to ensure every interaction is evidence‑based, age‑appropriate, and culturally sensitive, creating experiences that truly serve families.

Our Values

Safety First

Your child's safety, both digital and physical, is our top mission. From certified hardware construction to privacy‑first data handling, we never compromise on protecting what matters most.

Ethics at Our Core

We design with transparency, respect, and responsibility. Every decision prioritizes your family's wellbeing, ensures age‑appropriate content and gives you complete control over your child's experience.

Curiosity as a Compass

We believe children learn best when following their natural sense of wonder. We nurture curiosity by turning everyday moments into opportunities for exploration, questions and discovery.

Empowerment for All

We empower both children and parents. Children to explore confidently, and parents to guide meaningfully. Eddie removes the pressure of "doing it right" and builds your confidence as your child's most important teacher.
